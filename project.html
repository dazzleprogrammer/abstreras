Our Projects

Success Stories

Over the past few years, Absteras has had the privilege of working on more than 100 remarkable projects that have left an indelible mark on the world of exterior design. Our commitment to innovation, creativity, and excellence is reflected in each of these success stories.

Skyline Tower Revival

    Absteras was entrusted with the revival of the iconic Skyline Tower, a project that demanded not only the restoration of its historic facade but also the infusion of modernity. The result was a harmonious blend of old-world charm and contemporary elegance, earning accolades from both preservationists and modernists.

Eco-Oasis Residential Complex

    We designed a stunning residential complex that seamlessly integrated lush green spaces with cutting-edge architecture. The project's sustainable features, including green roofs and energy-efficient facades, garnered recognition as an environmentally conscious development.

Urban Art Installation

    Absteras collaborated with local artists to transform a drab city square into a vibrant urban art installation. The project brought a burst of color and creativity to the community, fostering a sense of identity and pride among residents. Corporate Innovation Hub: Our team crafted a futuristic facade for a corporate innovation hub, reflecting the company's commitment to cutting-edge technology. The project was featured in architectural publications for its avant-garde design and functional brilliance.

Historical Courtyard Restoration

    Absteras took on the challenging task of restoring a historical courtyard to its former glory. Meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to preserving the past while embracing the present resulted in a courtyard that now serves as a focal point for cultural events and gatherings.
